⚖️ Corporate Purposes & Statutory Powers

As defined in the Articles of Incorporation & By-Laws (SEC Approved), GEVI operates strictly within wholesale trading, energy value chain, and renewable energy development — without engaging in investment contracts with the public.

📦 Primary (Wholesale Trading)

Wholesale of solid, liquid and gaseous fuels and related products (BOC Import Permit: CCN IM0009636013). All kinds of goods, merchandise, export/import, consignment, distributor agreements and merchandise brokerage on fee/contract basis (Industry Code G461).

⛽ Secondary & Ancillary

Retail marketing, petroleum product distribution, operation of convenience stores supplementary to gasoline stations, domestic water transport & freight forwarding, and renewable energy project advisory. Independent Power Producer (IPP) and waste-to-energy consultancy.

🌱 Renewable & Green Direction

Renewable energy consultants, wind/solar farm development, green biomass, carbon credit advisory, and energy transition services — perfectly aligned with the primary purpose “goods of every kind and description” including energy commodities and sustainable infrastructure.

🔐 Important restriction

Corporation SHALL NOT solicit, accept or take investments/placements from the public nor issue investment contracts. No secondary license for securities dealing. This ensures compliance with SEC rules.

✅ Alignment with Executive Profile Summary: The company’s activities — Oil & Gas Trader, IPP, Logistics Manager, Commodity Trader, Renewable Energy Consultants — are perfectly underpinned by the AoI’s broad wholesale and distribution mandate, plus import-export powers. The “Energy Development and Consultancy, Geological Surveying, Depot Hub EPC” are derivative of the power to act as merchandise broker, commission merchant and to enter into service contracts for goods and related products.
